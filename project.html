BTS

BTS Countdown emerged as an engaging initiative to anticipate the return of members currently fulfilling their mandatory military service in South Korea. Drawing on my previous experience with API integration during the design of my world clock project, I eagerly embraced the opportunity to explore and apply my skills independently. The project allowed me to channel my excitement into creating a dynamic and personalized countdown experience, showcasing both my technical capabilities and enthusiasm for the meaningful return of the BTS members.
